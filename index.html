Second International Symposium on Computer Vision and Machine Intelligence in Medical Image Analysis (ISCMM-2021)

About ISCMM

Image processing and machine learning has made significant progress in the field of radiologic imaging. As for other sources of medical images research areas are nascent. The world of digital medical images includes acquiring, processing and visualizing structural changes from images of normal and diseased. The medical image processing includes extraction and processing of image-related information from any image modalities e.g. X-ray, CT, MRI etc most frequently and digital histopathology images in recent times. However, there many other imaging areas specialties like Ophthalmology (retinal and slit lamp images); ENT (Rhinoscopic, otoscopic, laryngoscopic images); Respiratory medicine (bronchoscopic images); Gastroenterology(endoscopic images); Gynaecology(colposcopic images); etc. Computer Vision and Machine intelligence in Medical images includes e.g. Filter, tissue-segmentation, image-registration, feature selection and disease classification. The machine learning area includes deep learning methodologies for medical image data analysis and statistical representation. Thus we need a formal meeting of various experts from both medical specialties and computer and technical specialities to come to a forum and discuss how to move forward in these challenging areas with new open questions.
